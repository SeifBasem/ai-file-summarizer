Artificial Intelligence is becoming an essential part of everyday life. Many businesses rely on AI to automate repetitive tasks and improve productivity. 
For example, chatbots are used in customer service to answer questions quickly and efficiently. AI is also used in smart devices like voice assistants and recommendation systems on streaming platforms.
Despite its benefits, there are still concerns about how AI systems make decisions and whether they are fair.
Researchers are working to improve transparency and reduce bias in AI models. 
As technology advances, AI will continue to influence how people work, communicate, and interact with digital systems.
